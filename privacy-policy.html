Privacy Policy

BeanFit  ·  Effective date: 8 June 2026  ·  Last updated: 8 June 2026

The short version: BeanFit does not collect, transmit, store on any server, or share any of your personal data. Everything you enter into the App stays on your device, under your control, at all times.

1. Who We Are

BeanFit is a personal fitness tracking application developed and operated by Anthony Grisoni, an individual based in the United Kingdom. The App is distributed through the Apple App Store.

2. Information We Collect

We collect no personal data.

All data you enter into BeanFit — including workout logs, exercise entries, sets, reps, weights, body weight records, session names, and any other information you record — is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's on-device storage (SwiftData). This data is never transmitted to any server, never accessed by the Developer, and never shared with any third party for any purpose.

We do not use any analytics tools, crash reporting services, advertising networks, or third-party SDKs that collect data. The App contains no tracking technology of any kind.

3. Information We Do Not Collect

BeanFit never collects:

  • Name, email address, or any contact information
  • Account credentials, passwords, or authentication data
  • Location data (GPS or otherwise)
  • Health, biometric, or fitness data transmitted off your device
  • Device identifiers (IDFA, IDFV, or similar)
  • IP addresses or network information
  • Usage analytics, session data, or behavioural data
  • Purchase history or payment information
  • Camera, microphone, photo library, contacts, or calendar data
  • Cookies, pixels, beacons, or any tracking technologies

4. How Your Data Is Stored

You own your data. All workout data is stored locally on your iPhone. You can export, back up, or delete your data at any time using the tools within the App. Uninstalling the App permanently removes all associated data.

iCloud Backup. If you have iCloud Backup enabled, Apple may include your BeanFit data in your device backup. This is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy (apple.com/legal/privacy) and is outside our control.

5. Third-Party Services

BeanFit does not integrate with any third-party services, APIs, or SDKs. Downloading the App requires an Apple ID and is subject to Apple's privacy policy. We have no access to any data Apple collects.

6. Children's Privacy

BeanFit is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Because we collect no personal information from any user, there is no specific privacy risk to younger users. If you are under 13, please do not use BeanFit.

8. State Privacy Rights (Other US States)

Various US state privacy laws (Virginia VCDPA, Colorado CPA, Connecticut CTDPA, and others) grant rights regarding personal data. Because BeanFit does not collect or process personal data, these laws do not impose obligations on us with respect to your use of the App.

9. Data Security

All data remains on your device. Its security is governed by your device's security features and Apple's on-device encryption. We recommend keeping your iOS version up to date and enabling device-level security (Face ID, Touch ID, passcode).

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in an updated version of the App and on this page. Your continued use of the App after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
